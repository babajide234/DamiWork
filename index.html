2022-2023 Dependent Verification Worksheet Instructions

For Priority Processing Submit Form within 15 business days

Failure to submit all documents together may result in a processing delay or less financial aid.

  1. Complete the Verification Worksheet and all additional information as requested
  2. Upload Tax Information or Verification of Non-Filing Status to Enterprise Self- Service Student Requirements under the Financial Aid tab.

2020 TAX FILERS

Parents and Students

IRS DATA RETRIEVAL TOOL (DRT) (PREFERED METHOD)

  • Sign into https.www.studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa
  • select parent/student finances page
  • select "link to IRS" and complete steps with IRS
  • Data will show as "Transferred from the IRS"

OR

SIGNED IRS FEDERAL TAX RETURN

  • Submit a signed copy of the 2020 IRS Federal Tax Return including IRS Schedules 1, 2, and 3
  • Include UIN on all pages of signed tax return

Section B – Household Information (Please read instructions below before completing)

Please list the people in your parent(s)’ household, including:

  1. yourself (even if you don’t live with your parents)
  2. your parent, or parents if married/living together, and/or step-parent if married according to FAFSA guidelines
  3. your parents’ other children if:
    • your parents will provide more than half of their support between July 1, 2022 and June 30, 2023 OR
    • the children would be required to provide parental information when applying for Federal Student Aid
  4. other dependents if:
    • they now live with your parents AND
    • your parents provide more than half of their support and will continue to provide more than half of their support between July 1, 2022 and June 30, 2023
Fill out the chart below – all fields must be completed.

Write the names of all household members as defined above. If you need more space, attach a separate sheet. Write in the name of the college for any household member listed (excluding parent(s)), who will be attending college at least half-time between July 1, 2022 and June 30, 2023, in a program that leads to a college degree/certificate. If the household member’s college is UIS, write their University Identification Number (UIN) in the box along with UIS listed as the college.
